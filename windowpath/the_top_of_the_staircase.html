The Top of the Staircase


You creak the door open and slip through quickly, making sure to be as quiet as possible. It leads to a hallway on the mansion's top floor, with a staircase down immediately to your left.

On the hallway to the right, however, is an item you recognize: the very same engine used in The Boom, the very first monster truck Jack Dynamite ever raced with. On the display is a red button labelled "Start Your Engines!", pulling you in to push it.
