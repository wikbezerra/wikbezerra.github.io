Festival

This festival is an invitation to not only simultaneously reconnect with one one another, but also to nature. Project Violet takes an ode to evaluate, educate, and elongate environmental stewardship and our sense of responsibility to protecting environmental quality.

“Recycled” art pieces also doubled as seating- repurposed wood and plastic water bottles and crates.



Mission Statement

We are committed to the development of the artist community and to personal growth. We make fascinating cross-connections between art, technolgy, the self, and the natural world. Allowing attendees to walk away armed with knowledge and tools to make decisions to benefit society and the planet.
