The Sound of the Century


Banging on the gong, a massive sound starts to resonate throughout the house. Quickly, you hear murmuring and footsteps upstairs. When the noise starts to centralize around the top of the staircase into the Manor itself, you duck behind the nearby wall as guards begin to crash downstairs.

Once all the guards are downstairs, they begin crowding around the gong, examining it closely and leaving all other parts of the room unoccupied.
